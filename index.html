About Meaford DJ

With over 10,000 events and 25 years in business, Meaford DJ is a trusted and award-winning DJ service in Ontario. Our team tailors every event to your unique mood and taste, offering an extensive variety of music spanning from the 1940s to today's Top 40 hits.

Our reputation is built on outstanding service, reasonable prices, and a vast music selection, making us a top choice in Meaford and beyond. Recommended by previous clients, venues, and event guests, Meaford DJ is proud to be a member of the Canadian Disc Jockey Association (CDJA), AVLA licensed, and fully insured.
